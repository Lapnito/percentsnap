## What is PercentSnap?

**PercentSnap** is a focused, fast percentage calculator for the things you actually need to compute in everyday life: discounts at the store, sale prices, markup and margin for pricing, VAT and tax, percent change, reverse percentage to find an original value, percentage points, tip splitting, compound growth, and what fraction of a total a number represents.

It's built for quick answers without clutter. Open the app, tap the calculator you need, type the numbers, and read the result. There are no ads, no sign-up, and no tracking — your history, pins and settings stay on your device.

## Key Features

### Calculators
- **Discount calculator** — sale price and savings from an original price
- **Percent change** — percentage increase or decrease between two numbers
- **Reverse percentage** — find the original value before a percentage was applied
- **Original price calculator** — what something was before a discount
- **Markup calculator** — selling price from cost and markup
- **Margin calculator** — margin and profit from cost and selling price
- **VAT / tax calculator** — add or remove VAT or sales tax
- **Percentage point calculator** — difference in percentage points
- **Percent of number** — what is X% of Y
- **What percent is** — what percent is X of Y
- **Required percentage change** — the percentage change needed to go from A to B
- **Tip and split calculator** — tip plus bill split among friends
- **Compound growth calculator** — compound annual growth over time
- **Portion of total** — part as a percentage of total

### History and convenience
- **Local calculation history** — every calculation is saved on your device
- **Pin frequently used calculations** for quick recall
- **One-tap copy** of results to clipboard
- **Share or export** history when you need it
- **Adjustable formatting and rounding** settings

### Privacy
- **No ads** — none, anywhere in the app
- **No tracking** — no analytics, no third-party SDKs
- **No account** — no sign-up, no email, no login required
- **On-device** — history, pins, and settings stay on your device

## How PercentSnap Works

**Q: Why use a dedicated percentage app instead of the system calculator?**
A: System calculators don't have purpose-built UIs for "discount", "VAT off", "reverse percentage", or "tip and split". PercentSnap labels every input clearly and shows the formula it used so you don't have to remember which buttons to press.

**Q: Are calculations done online?**
A: No. Every calculation is done on the device. The app does not require an internet connection.

**Q: Can I save common calculations?**
A: Yes. Pin a calculation you use often and it stays at the top for one-tap recall.

**Q: Does it work offline?**
A: Fully. The app does not need internet for any feature.

## FAQ

**Is PercentSnap free?**
Yes. No ads, no in-app purchases, no subscriptions.

**Do I need an account?**
No. There is no sign-up, login, or email field anywhere in the app.

**Does it collect data?**
No. Calculation history, pins, and settings are stored on your device only.

**Can it handle currencies and rounding?**
Yes. The settings let you adjust decimal places and number formatting to match your country.

**Is it just for shopping?**
No. It covers shopping (discounts, sale price), business (markup, margin, VAT), finance (compound growth, percent change), and everyday math (tip, split, percent of total).

**Why does it need internet permission?**
It doesn't. The app does not require internet.

**What devices are supported?**
Any Android phone or tablet, and iPhones / iPads on iOS 13.0 or later.

**How accurate is the math?**
PercentSnap performs standard floating-point arithmetic and shows the exact formula it used so you can verify the result.

**Can I export my calculation history?**
Yes. You can share or export the history when you need it.

**How do I report a bug?**
Open an issue at [github.com/Lapnito/percentsnap/issues](https://github.com/Lapnito/percentsnap/issues) or email tom@lapnito.cz.
